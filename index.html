BEER PONG FOR NERDS

The classic numbers game for math bros.

Here's how it works:

First, grab a cold beer. Then enter a number. Sit back and enjoy as your buddy Beer-Bot counts up to your number starting from 1, with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 = "BEER!"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 = "PONG!"
  3. Numbers divisible by both 3 & 5 = "BEER-PONG!"
    Drink up bro!

It's almost like you're really playing beer pong . . . only you're all alone and this game makes no sense . . .
