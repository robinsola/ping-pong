BEER PONG FOR NERDS

The classic numbers game for math bros.

How it works:

Beer-Bot will count up to your number starting from 1, with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 = "BEER!"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 = "PONG!"
  3. Numbers divisible by both 3 & 5 = "BEER-PONG!"
